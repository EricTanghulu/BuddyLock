What BuddyLock Does

BuddyLock combines focus protection, social accountability, challenges, and game-like motivation into one iOS experience.

Focus Sessions

Reduce impulsive app use with more intentional structure.

  • Shield selected apps, categories, and domains
  • Warm-up timer before the session starts
  • Scheduled focus sessions
  • Automatic logging of completed focus minutes

Challenges

Compete with friends or join app-wide events for rewards.

  • Friend-based usage challenges
  • Global community challenge participation
  • Rewards, punishments, and in-game currency
  • Leaderboard-based motivation

Friends & Accountability

Use trusted friends as real support when temptation hits.

  • Unlock requests inside chats
  • Optional small tasks before approval
  • Override path for urgent access
  • Shared environments and virtual pets

Technology

Built for iOS with a product direction centered around focus management, accountability, and a polished native experience.

SwiftUI

Modern native UI for a responsive and cohesive iOS product.

FamilyControls & ManagedSettings

Supports app blocking, shielding, and screen-time based workflows.

Evolving Product

Designed to grow from a focused prototype into a richer social accountability platform.

Roadmap

BuddyLock is being developed toward a fuller experience with stronger social features, insights, and longer-term progression systems.

Cloud Sync

Future synchronization across devices and more persistent account data.

Richer Social Features

Posts, stories, challenge proof, and more polished sharing moments.

Expanded Progression

More rewards, environment building, pet interactions, and challenge depth.
