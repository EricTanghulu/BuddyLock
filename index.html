BuddyLock

BuddyLock is an iOS app prototype developed by BuddyLock LLC. It helps users limit screen time and build healthier focus habits through structured focus sessions and social accountability.

Features

  • Focus Sessions
    • Shield selected apps, categories, and domains
    • Warm-up timer to resist impulsive unlocks
    • Scheduled focus sessions
    • Automatic logging of completed focus minutes
  • Buddy System
    • Add and manage trusted buddies
    • Send unlock requests for accountability
    • Approve or deny requests
    • Temporary exceptions that automatically expire
  • Challenges
    • Head-to-head and group challenges
    • Leaderboards based on focus minutes
    • Manual logging support

Technology

  • SwiftUI
  • FamilyControls & ManagedSettings
  • Local persistence (UserDefaults)
  • No cloud sync yet (CloudKit planned)

Roadmap

  • CloudKit synchronization
  • Advanced challenge statistics
  • Motivational nudges and reminders
  • Improved app name resolution
